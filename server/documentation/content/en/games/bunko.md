\*\*Bunko\*\*

Bunko is a fast social dice game driven by momentum, luck, and daring streaks of good rolls. Each round revolves around one target number, and the table races to score against that number before the round closes.

\*\*Gameplay\*\*

\* Bunko uses \*\*three six-sided dice\*\*.
\* The game is played over a set number of rounds. By default, that is \*\*6 rounds\*\*.
\* Each round has a \*\*target number\*\*. Round 1 targets 1s, round 2 targets 2s, and so on through 6.
\* On your turn, you roll all three dice.
\* If you score, you keep rolling.
\* If you score nothing, your turn ends immediately and play passes to the next player.

\*\*Special Mechanics\*\*

\* A round ends as soon as a player reaches or exceeds \*\*21 points\*\* for that round.
\* A Bunko also ends the round immediately, because it is worth 21 points on its own.
\* After a round ends, the player sitting to the \*\*left of the round winner\*\* starts the next round.
\* If the host chooses more than 6 rounds, the target numbers continue cycling from 1 back to 6.

\*\*Scoring\*\*

\* \*\*1 point\*\* for each die that matches the current round's target number.
\* \*\*Mini Bunko:\*\* all three dice match each other but do \*not\* match the round's target number. This scores \*\*5 points\*\*.
\* \*\*Bunko:\*\* all three dice match the round's target number. This scores \*\*21 points\*\* and ends the round immediately.

\*\*Winning The Match\*\*

The match winner depends on the selected \*\*Winning Mode\*\*:

\* \*\*Round Wins:\*\* the player with the most rounds won is ranked highest.
\* \*\*Total Score:\*\* the player with the highest total points across the whole match is ranked highest.

Ties are broken by the remaining tracked stats in this order:

\* total points
\* Bunko count
\* mini Bunko count

\*\*Customizable Options\*\*

\* \*\*Rounds:\*\* Default is 6. The host can choose from 1 to 12 rounds.
\* \*\*Winning Mode:\*\* Choose whether the final winner is decided by round wins or by total score.

\*\*Keyboard Shortcuts\*\*

\* \*\*R:\*\* Roll the dice.
\* \*\*E:\*\* Hear the current round, target number, and table leader.
\* \*\*D:\*\* Hear the latest roll and its result.
\* \*\*T:\*\* Check whose turn it is.
\* \*\*S:\*\* Check the standings.
\* \*\*Ctrl+U:\*\* Hear who is at the table.
